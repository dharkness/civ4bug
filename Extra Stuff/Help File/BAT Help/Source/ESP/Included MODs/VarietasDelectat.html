Varietas Delectat

by Avain


This mod is based on the fabulous GeoModder's "Ethnic Artstyles" mod and the brilliant WhiteRabbit's "Ethnically Diverse Units Mod" mod.

The aim of the mod is to create as ethnically diverse unit sets as possible, to the extent that in most cases not only the cultural/racial groups were the bases of these unit sets, but a single Civilization.

As with projects of this scope, it's far from being perfect, and some/all decisions of unit use can be (are?) questionable.

Varietas Delectat includes the following mods:
  • Ethnic Artstyles by GeoModder (now Ethnic Citystyles) which extends the cultural diversity to city styles
  • Cultural Diversity by Chugginator which was an independent mod of Varietas Delectat) adding more Units
