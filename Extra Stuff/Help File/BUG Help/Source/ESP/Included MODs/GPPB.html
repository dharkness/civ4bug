Great Person Progress Bar by Impaler[WrG]

 

About Great Person Progress Bar

The Great Person Progress Bar adds a bar below your Technology Research bar. If in wide screen mode it will display to the right of the Research bar. It tells you how many turns until your next Great Person, as well as what type of Great Person is likely to be Born.

 

Instructions

From within The BUG Options Screen, on the General Tab, you can turn the Great Person Bar on or off. You can also toggle the display mode to show only the most likely Great Person, to listing all of the Great People that city could have born, with the percentage of the change for that type of Great Person.
