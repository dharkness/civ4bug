Raw Yields by EmperorFool

 

Instructions

You can enable or disable this mod using the BUG Options screen's (Ctrl-Alt-O) General tab. Simply toggle the "Raw Yield " check box in the first column under "City Screen". You can switch between the display of either Production, Commerce,or Food, by clicking on it's icon in the heard of the Yields display in the upper left quarter of the City Screen.

 

Raw Yields

Raw Yields allows you to display a detailed breakdown of each of the tree production type: Commerce, Produce, and Food. To toggle between the modes, click on the icon representing each yield type in the Header. It will give you a detailed breakdown of what each raw yield is, as well as what the actual value is after modifiers.

It will add up the yields for worked tiles, all tiles in the cities control, or all tiles culturally owned. It will also eliminate the display of 0 values, to help reduce clutter and keep the list of buildings in the city as large as possible. Everything is displayed in a table format, and should be fairly easy to see what the raw yield a city is capable of as soon as you build it, so you know where to focus your improvements, and ensure your city is giving the most possible for your needs.

 

This mod was based of the original Raw Commerce Mod by Sevo.
