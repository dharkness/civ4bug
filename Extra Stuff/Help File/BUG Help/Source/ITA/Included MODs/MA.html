About the BUG Military Advisor

The Military Advisor is now divided into two sections, Deployment, and the Sit-Rep (or Situation Report).

The Deployment tab makes finding units easier by grouping units by location and unit type.

The Sit-Rep tab is your all-in-one spot to find information related to your warmonging diplomacy. It displays the follow information:

  • Icon for each Civilization in the game.
  • A fist indicating that the Civilization "Has Enough on Their Hands", which usually means they are planning, or are engaged in a war that is taking all their resources.
  • An icon display who the Civilzations Worst Enemy is.
  • A Threat Indicator. This is not perfect, but gives you a rough idea what the threat level of attack from that civilization is towards you.
  • Columns showing Strategic Advantages, both yours and the other civlizations. This is useful so you don't go attacking rifles with swordsman!
  • An indicator for if that civilization is a vassal of another.
  • An indicator if the civilization has a defensive pact.
