General Tab

 

Main Interface:

Great Person Progress Bar:

Toggles the bar below the Research Bar that displays current progress towards your next Great Person. You can change the amount of information displayed on the GP Bar using the drop-down menu titled GP Bar Types.

 

Combat Experience:

If checked, the Combat Experience counter from the F5 screen is displayed next to the GP Bar on the Main Screen.

 

City Cycle Arrows:

Toggles the City Cycle Arrows. These are displayed nex to the End Turn button on the Main Screen.

 

Promotion Available:

Will turn on the Promotion display within Improved Unit Icons.

 

Unit Actions:

If checked, a unit that has a pre-defined action (heal, goto, automated, fortified, etc) has text describing the action added to the bottom of its unit icon.

 

 

City Screens:

Raw Commerce / Production:

This option enables the Raw Commerce & Raw Production mod.

 

Culture Turns:

Will display the number of turns until cultural growth in the culture bar.

Great Person Turns:

Will display the number of turns until the next Great Person in the Great Person bar within the City Screen.

 

Options Shortcut Reminder:

Will change the blinking "Press Enter to end turn" to "Press Ctrl-Alt-O to customize BUG Mod"

 

Anger Counter:

When Checked will Enable the Anger Counter, which tells you how long until unhapiness will end from using population to hurry production.
