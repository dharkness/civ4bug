Instructions

You can enable or disable this mod using the BUG Options screen's (Ctrl-Alt-O) General tab. Simply toggle the "Raw Commerce / Production" checkbox in the first column under "City Screen". You can switch between the display of Raw Commerce or Raw Production by clicking on the Coin or the Hammer in the City Screen, in the upper left quarter of the City Screen.

 

Raw Commerce

It bugged me that there was no way to view the raw commerce being produced in your city without scrolling over one of the science/culture/coin icons. Even then you only got the "total" raw commerce without getting an easy display of the breakdown. So I added another panel below the commerce panel to show the raw city commerce. - Sevo

 

Raw Production

This mod extends Sevo's Raw Commerce mod by adding a button to toggle between displaying raw commerce and raw production. The button is the hammer icon when viewing commerce and the coin icon when viewing production. In other words, it shows you what it will switch to when you press it. I also modified Raw Commerce to show the 50% capital bonus under the Bureaucracy civic.

Note that the bonuses are difficult to detail using the limited space of this mod. It excludes bonuses due to the nature of the item you are producing, e.g. 100% for access to stone or 50%/100% for military units. The idea is to show you the raw production you'll get when producing any type of item in that city.
