Scoreboard Options

 

Display Name:

You can toggle between what name is displayed in the scored. Either the Leader's name, the Civilization's name, or both the Leader and the Civilizations name.

 

Attitude Icons:

Will toggle between Attitude Icons being displayed or not.

 

Dead Civilizations:

You can show Civilizations that have been destroyed in different ways. You can either show them or remove them. You can add a tag to their name saying they are "Dead", or you can gray out the name. You can use any combination of these thee options you'd like.

 

Power Ratio:

This will display the Power Ratio in the Score.

Default Color:

The color to display when your power ratio is even with another civilization.

Good Ratio Cutoff:

The point at which the ratio changes from even to good.

Good Color:

The color to display when your power ratio is stronger then another civilization.

Bad Ration Cutoff:

The point at which the ratio changes from even to bad.

Bad Color:

The color to display when your power ratio is weaker then another civilization.

 

Advanced Layout:

This will give you more options to change the layout of the Scoreboard. Select the Enabled Box to turn these extra options on.

Column Order:

This will give you complete control over what is, or isn't displayed in the score. You can also change the order of what is displayed here as well. The items you can display in the Scoreboard are:

  • S - The civ's score.

  • C - The civ's/leader's name.

  • ? - You have not yet met the civ.

  • W - You are at war with the civ.

  • E - You have a positive espionage point ratio against the civ.

  • P - The civ's power ratio compared to you.

  • T - The tech the civ is researching (vassals, teammates and espionage).

  • U - The number of research turns left.

  • N - The civ is connected to your trade network.

  • B - You have an open borders agreement with the civ.

  • D - You have a defensive pact with the civ.

  • R - The civ's state religion.

  • A - The civ's attitude toward you.

  • * - You are waiting for this civ to finish its turn.

  • L - civ's network stats (ping).

  • O - The network player is out-of-sync.

Left Align Name:

This aligns the civilizations name so it is to the left, instead of the right.

Research Icons:

This will display the icon of the technology the other civilization is researching instead of the name of the technology.
