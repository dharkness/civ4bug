Advisors Options

Domestic Advisor

Customizable:

This option turns on the Customizable Domestic Advisor.

 

 

Foreign Advisor

Glance Tab:

This re-enables the Glance Tab which was removed with version 3.13 of Beyond the Sword.

Glance Tab Smilies:

This turns on color coded smiles, making it easier to see other Civilizations attitudes towards you and others.

 

 

Technology Advisor

Great Person Research:

This will add icons to the Technology Advisor screen that will tell you what technology will be given to you depending on what type of Great Person is used to discover it.

 

 

Sevopedia

 

Enabled:

Will turn Sevopedia on or off.

Sort List:

Will change the order the entries are shown.

 

Espionage

Better Espionage:

Enables the Better Espionage Screen

 

Ratio Color:

What color to display the Espionage Ratio when the ratio is even.

Good Ratio Cutoff:

The percentage when the Espionage ratio changes from even to good.

Good Color:

What color to display the Espionage Ratio when the ratio is good.

Bad Ratio Cutoff:

The percentage when the Espionage ratio changes from even to bad.

Bad Color:

What color to display the Espionage Ratio when the ratio is good.

 

Possible Mission Color:

Choose the color you want to use to know when you can perform a mission against another civilization.

Close Percent Cutoff:

This will let you decide at what level to change the Close Mission Color. 5% means you are 5% away from the mission being possible.

Close Mission Color:

Choose the color you want to use to know when you are close to being able to perform a mission against another civilization.
