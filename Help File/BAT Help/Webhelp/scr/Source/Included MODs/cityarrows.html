City Cycle Arrows by SimCutie

 

About City Cycle Arrows

This Mod will allow you to cycle through your cities from the Main Interface screen.

 

Instructions

Above your minimap in the Main Interface screen are a white left and right arrow. Clicking one of them will do a single-click action (pulling up city information in main interface, without detailed citizen information,) on the closest city in the main view.
